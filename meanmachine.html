Background



Mean Machine was meant as a module in a bigger website - citivelocity.com. The Mean Machine is an  analytical tool that algorithmically searches for interesting trading opportunities. It presents the users with assets that are trading away from their historical pattern, and potentially could be a profit opportunity.


“Hey Nati, the Apple stock would be a good buy today. Why? Because it’s trading at an X level. It is pretty low for Apple, and historically it has always went up after Y days. You should buy it now!     ”

Ideation and Concept Design
