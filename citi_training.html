Background



Citi Bank is a huge organization with over 200,000 employees, which are required to pass certification training sessions, as well as optional sessions for performance improve and personal enrichment. The current training platform does not support that, and its usability is already clumsy and discouraging further exploration.

Challenge

Creating a training platform for all Citi employees that better supports completion of mandatory training, encourages users to do more training

for personal interest and career projection, as well as providing visibility to managers on their employees' status.

In order to better understand the needs and head-start the project, we worked on several fronts:

 - Interviews to training department stakeholders

 - Interviews to employees in the Citi Innovation Lab as users

 - Interviews to managers to better understand the visibility needs


We created persona document according to these three user types.


Also, I created a micro-copy guide for the new learning system, aimed to give a consistent personality to the system.

The system should be friendly, encouraging, optimistic, clear on its status and pending tasks. The system's message should be "first the must, and then the other".


Lastly, I explored deeply the area of gamification and how could it help on the engagement of the users.

The training project was legendary for our team! Not only it was our first shot at Sketch and Zeplin, but also we first played around with Material Design and its style.


The font selected was Roboto, as it is clear and readable on screen, ideal for modern web apps. We kept the Citi blue wave in order to align with the brand's spirit.


We experimented also with spacing and achieved a lighthearted style that contrasts with most enterprise interfaces, usually cramped and not breathing.
